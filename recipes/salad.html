Salad

This is a salad common with hispanic culture.

It's a very refreshing side to go with chips, meat and or even by itself!

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion
  • 1 cilantro bunch
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 limes
  • 2 chile serranos
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Dice onion.
  2. Mince cilantro into small pieces.
  3. Cut tomatoes into halves and clean out the mush.
  4. Dice what's left of the remaining tomatoes.
  5. Cut chile serranos top end and clean out the seeds.
  6. Chop serranos.
  7. Mix the vegetables.
  8. Salt and pepper the salad with a pinch of each.
  9. squeeze limes into the salad then thoroughly mix.
